HOW TO PREPARE FOR 10TH BOARD?

November 14,2021

HOW TO PREPARE FOR 10TH BOARD?
Hello everyone! Today we are going to talk on the topic on which most people get stuck for the preparation of 10th board. This article will be helpful to all students of 10th standard. So let’s get started on the amazing topic.
How to prepare for the 10th board?
For preparing for the board you must not feel pressure of the board or of 10th .It will be easy to get good marks in 10th just by clearing your concepts. You can also refer to our article i.e. does 10th marks matter it will know does it matter to you or not? Now let’s get started with the topic.
1. Make a time table of your daily schedule:
Timetables will help you to manage your whole day and make your study time easy. Making a study time of 2-3 hours daily is enough to study.
2. Go through Syllabus:
Go through Syllabus and note down the chapter that you are left with and then make a timetable of it.
3. Do Revision what you study whole day:
Do revision whatever you study for the whole day including revision of your school and coaching. It will make your study easy.
4. Focus on concepts:
You should focus more on understanding the concepts. It will make it easy to answer the questions of the exam and you will be able to write answers in your own words.
5. Go through previous year
Previous year questions are must do because you get the knowledge of the recently asked questions and most asked questions and somehow contribute 70percent to your marks.
6. Refer high level of books
Referring books can improve your knowledge and concept deeply. So that if the question is out of syllabus then you will be easily able to answer the question as you already know the concepts.
7. Take break at certain interval:
The importance that you gave to the study should be the same as recess because recess is also a part of study and to make your mind relax.
8. Do Exercise daily:
Daily exercise is also needed for good health. If your health is good then only you will be able to study well. You can join yoga or join a gym or do some basic level of exercise at home.
9. Sleep:
Sleep is the most important factor. You should sleep at least 6 -8 hours daily to remain healthy and to keep your mind fresh.
10. Use table and Chair:
For study use a table and chair to concentrate on studies because on bed you will feel laziness due to which you will feel sleepiness and not able to study more.
11. Weak and Strong Subject:
Find out your strong and weak subjects to make your study easy and fun. Try to Study weak subjects in the morning and strong subjects in boring time.
12. Techniques to study:
You can also study by using some techniques like the 50-10 rule. This rule is like studying for 50 minutes and taking a break of 10 minutes. It is one of the effective methods of study. You can also find out some more techniques on YouTube and Google. Studying by writing the answers means writing the answers to whatever topic you study so that you will be able to learn it fastly and it will improve your handwriting too.
Note: Enjoy the study not a burden, it will make you easy to study.
So these were some tips or strategies to follow to get good marks in 10th board. I hope all of you enjoyed the article .Thanks for reading the article and Best of Luck for the exams.
