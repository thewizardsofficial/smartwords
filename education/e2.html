Does 10th marks Really matter?

- DHANISH PATEL
November 14,2021

Today’s topic is a must read for all students to those who are in 10th or just completed the 10th and waiting for the result. Before sliding into this topic we suggest you check out our other articles also.
Is Success in Life = Marks in 10th ???
We may hear our parents or elders say that 10th marks are very important for your career and life. But a natural question arises in our mind if this is true or not? Actually 10th marks are not the important factor for career because it’s not the last exam or last opportunity in your life to get success. There is an ocean of opportunities in life. Life gives us many chances to pursue our passion or to get success. So why should we even try to get good marks?
No matter what field you choose or what career you pursue there is no shortcut for success. You need to have focus and hard-work to achieve something. If you work hard for the 10th Boards exam then in this process you learn about competition out there, about persistence and working hard for goals.
Places where Class 10th marks matter:
1. It will be required to get admission in high school i.e. in 11th-science, commerce, arts or even in diploma.
2. If you are thinking of going to go foreign university for higher studies then they also consider 10th result in the criteria to get admission. Even in India also, IIMs give some weightage to 10th marks.
3. When you send a resume to the recruiter for the placement then it would create a good impression if you have a nice result in 10th.
4. In many government exams selection also the 10th marks are considered.
Hence, these are the places where 10th marks hold value and that may affect your career.
So at last, I would say just work hard without any expectations and don’t be tense even if the result is not good. There will be many more opportunities in life.
I hope you get the answer to the question. Have a nice day ahead and good luck for your future.
