ONLINE VS OFFLINE LEARNINGS

- DHANISH PATEL
November 14,2021

ONLINE VS OFFLINE LEARNINGS
The famous topic to discuss for students during this pandemic situation. Let’s go ahead and talk about the pros and cons of both side.
Emerging of Online Lecture:
Due to the covid-19 which spread from China in December 2019 and spread into the whole world becomes the reason for the emerging of the online lecture and learning. Now a days the use of online platform has increased a lot.
Is it Good to have online lectures?
Yes now a days students prefer online studies rather than offline because they get comfortable and have flexibility to learn from anywhere with anybody posture. Moreover their attendance issue is also solved in online by just entering the lecture during the attendance time.
Advantages of Online Lectures:
It is flexible; can be accessed from anywhere with a device and internet connection. Budget-friendly.
Convenient attendance. Class recordings are available for later references.
Disadvantages of Online Lectures:
Though student can learn from online but the practical knowledge is not getting more in online. Due to which they lack in their practical knowledge.
They have to constantly look on the screen which damage their eyes.
They cannot makes friends and have school/ college time with friends.
Studying in online is tougher because there are lots of distraction in mobile. Makes tough for teachers to explain the concept.
Advantages of Offline classes:
You can easily understand the concept and can concentrate more compare to online. Doubts can be easily solved.
Teachers can get know the status of all students easily.
Disadvantages of offline classes:
Time management becomes tough.
Less introduced to latest technology.
At last it will be good enough to attend offline learning then online because there are more advantages and less disadvantages of offline.
