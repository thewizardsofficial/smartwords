How to follow your Passion along with studies?

- By Mihir Gandhi
November 14,2021

Hello guys today we are going to talk about what passion is and how to follow it along with studies and many more. So let’s get started.
How to find passion within us?
First of all let us see what passion actually means? So passion is like having interest in something with which you are fully attached i.e. you can do these for the whole life without getting bored. Sometimes people are not able to differentiate between addiction and passion.
Addiction is the over exceeded use of that thing in which the person cannot live without it.For today’s generation children get addicted to games, social media and many more which is not good. Making passion an addiction is also good but up to some level because more access to anything can cause harm to us. Having passion is good but addiction is not good. So addiction is another topic and is different from passion.
Difference between successful people and unsuccessful people
Successful people know their passion from their childhood and work towards it from childhood whereas unsuccessful people don’t know their passion or they know then also they don’t give time for the passion or not work towards it.Moreover another difference is about the speed of action.Successful people works faster on the idea they get and unsuccessful person will not works towards it for most of the time or make actions later.
How to follow passion along with studies?
So first of all many children make excuses that they don’t get time to follow their passion due to school homework and other stuff. So I would like to recommend you not to make excuses or make excuses to not to make excuses.
Now make a timetable of your whole day with a scheduled time of your passion when you can passionately follow your passion without any excuses and give at least 2-3 hour a day to make your passion up to a greater height. You should also give some study time to your school work so that it can be easily managed. Moreover 6-8 hours’ sleep should also be there in the timetable.
How can parents support their children for their passion?
Parents are requested to support their child for their passion in the childhood because without parents support it becomes harder for the child to achieve a high level of achievements in their passion. Moreover their interest towards their passion can also decrease due to no support from parents. So I request all parents to support their child for their good future.
At last all should follow your passion and be passionate about It.Thanks for reading the article. Have a great future ahead!
